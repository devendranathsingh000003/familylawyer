Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a divorce case take in Rewa?

Mutual consent divorce can be completed in 6-8 months. Contested divorces may take 1-2 years depending on case complexity.

What are the grounds for divorce in MP?

Common grounds include cruelty, desertion, adultery, and mutual consent. Each case is unique and requires specific legal approach.

How is maintenance amount calculated?

Maintenance depends on factors like income, standard of living, and dependents' needs. We help ensure fair maintenance claims.
